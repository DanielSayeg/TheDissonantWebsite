About

Mauricio Gonzalez

Mauricio Gonzalez is a freelance artist with a passion for storytelling and world building. He runs on caffeine, and if he’s not holding a pencil, he is playing with his dogs or covered in clay.

He has been visually and narratively developing worlds ever since he could hold a pencil — The Dissonant is neither his first nor last.

Daniel Sayeg

Daniel Sayeg is a game designer by morning and a graphic novelist by dawn. He’s into cartoons, punk music, and lately making tortellini from scratch.

He had the concepts from the world of The Dissonant in his head for years, and wrote them into his short stories and his D&D campaigns. The story and the characters are inspired by his political beliefs and struggles, and the people in his life.

Together

We met as foreign art students at École Émile Cohl in Lyon, France. Though neither of us finished our art degree, we stayed in touch, collaborating on comics and game jams.

The Dissonant is our most ambitious project yet.

Cast

Aashvi Unba

Aashvi is the brains of the dynamic duo between her and her brother. She is an introverted girl who prefers books over swords, which often made her an outsider to her Bone-Born friends. Knowing how her surroundings react to her intellectual tendencies, Aashvi found peace in her family’s side.

Duru Unba

When Aashvi stops to think, Duru takes careless action. He is the younger, restless sibling of the two who dreams of bringing honour to his family and becoming an elite soldier, a true Bone-Born. But for now, he is too young to be marked – and before the days of chivalry arrive, he enjoys the days of mischief.

Panash Unba

Often being mocked for working as an electrician instead of a proper Bone-Born job, Panash’s inner peace is hard to shake; and the root of that peace is his responsibility of fatherhood. Being both the caretaker and provider for his two children is not an easy task, and yet it is one that he does proudly.
